Egypt and the Suez corridor suffer a nationalist revolt and sabotage campaign that draws in multiple powers. Suez is extremely valuable to the global powers because of its access to the [[Mediterranean]]. But any Axis-aligned “protectorate” would be seen as humiliating to the Egyptians. Domestic strikes, bombings, mutinies, and a coup are followed by external intervention to “secure the canal.”
##### Inspiration
- Egyptian nationalism (including the Free Officers)
- Suez Crisis dynamics
- canal-zone insurgency/sabotage patterns.
